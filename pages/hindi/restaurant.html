Common Conversations in a Restaurant

Common Conversations in a Restaurant:

  • Can I see the menu, please? - Kya main menu dekh sakta hoon?
  • What are today's specials? - Aaj ke khaas vyanjan kya hain?
  • Do you have vegetarian options? - Kya aapke paas vegetarian options hain?
  • Can I get a table for two? - Kya mujhe do logon ke liye table mil sakta hai?
  • Is this dish spicy? - Kya yeh vyanjan teekha hai?
  • Can I get a glass of water? - Kya mujhe ek glass paani mil sakta hai?
  • Can you recommend something? - Kya aap kuch recommend kar sakte hain?
  • Is there a kids' menu available? - Kya yahaan bachchon ka menu available hai?
  • Do you have any vegan dishes? - Kya aapke paas koi vegan dishes hain?
  • Can I have the bill, please? - Kya mujhe bill mil sakta hai?
  • Is there a service charge included? - Kya ismein service charge shamil hai?
  • Can I pay by card? - Kya main card se payment kar sakta hoon?
  • Do you serve alcohol? - Kya yahaan alcohol milta hai?
  • Where is the restroom? - Restroom kahaan hai?
  • Can I reserve a table? - Kya main ek table reserve kar sakta hoon?
  • Is there a buffet option? - Kya yahaan buffet ka option hai?
  • Can I have some extra napkins? - Kya mujhe kuch extra napkins mil sakte hain?
  • Is the food halal? - Kya yahan ka khana halal hai?
  • What are your opening hours? - Aapke opening hours kya hain?
  • Can I have this dish without onions? - Kya yeh vyanjan bina pyaz ke ban sakta hai?
  • Do you offer home delivery? - Kya aap home delivery karte hain?
  • Do you have gluten-free options? - Kya aapke paas gluten-free options hain?
  • Can I have a bottle of mineral water? - Kya mujhe ek bottle mineral water mil sakta hai?
  • Is there a waiting time for a table? - Kya table ke liye wait karna padega?
  • Can you split the bill? - Kya aap bill split kar sakte hain?
  • What is the signature dish here? - Yahan ka signature dish kya hai?
  • Do you serve dessert? - Kya aap dessert serve karte hain?
  • Can I get a takeaway box? - Kya mujhe ek takeaway box mil sakta hai?
  • Do you have a salad bar? - Kya yahan salad bar hai?
  • Is there a dress code? - Kya yahaan dress code hai?
  • Do you have outdoor seating? - Kya aapke paas outdoor seating hai?
  • Can I see the drinks menu? - Kya main drinks ka menu dekh sakta hoon?
  • Can I get some extra ketchup? - Kya mujhe kuch extra ketchup mil sakta hai?
  • Do you accept UPI payments? - Kya aap UPI payment accept karte hain?
  • Is there any discount for students? - Kya students ke liye koi discount hai?
  • Can I change my order? - Kya main apna order change kar sakta hoon?
  • What time does the kitchen close? - Kitchen kitne baje band hoti hai?
  • Can I get a quiet table? - Kya mujhe ek shaant jagah table mil sakti hai?
  • Do you offer group bookings? - Kya aap group bookings karte hain?
  • What drinks do you have? - Aapke paas kaun kaun si drinks hain?
  • Is there free Wi-Fi here? - Kya yahan free Wi-Fi hai?
  • Do you have a bar counter? - Kya aapke paas bar counter hai?
  • Can you pack this food? - Kya aap is khane ko pack kar sakte hain?
  • Do you provide high chairs for kids? - Kya aap bachchon ke liye high chair dete hain?
  • Is tipping mandatory? - Kya tipping zaroori hai?
  • Do you have live music? - Kya yahan live music hai?
  • Can I order from the chef’s special? - Kya main chef’s special se order kar sakta hoon?
  • Is this a family-friendly restaurant? - Kya yeh family-friendly restaurant hai?
  • Can you adjust the spice level? - Kya aap spice level adjust kar sakte hain?
  • Do you have allergy-friendly dishes? - Kya aapke paas allergy-friendly dishes hain?
