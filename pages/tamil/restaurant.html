Common Conversations in a Restaurant

Common Conversations in a Restaurant:

  • Can I see the menu, please? - Enakku menu kaamikkalamaa?
  • What are today's specials? - Innaikku enna special dishes irukku?
  • Do you have vegetarian options? - Unga kitta vegetarian options irukkaa?
  • Can I get a table for two? - Enakku rendu perukku table kidaikkuma?
  • Is this dish spicy? - Indha dish kaaram irukkaa?
  • Can I get a glass of water? - Enakku oru glass thanni kidaikkuma?
  • Can you recommend something? - Neenga edhavathu recommend panna mudiyumaa?
  • Is there a kids' menu available? - Inga kuzhandhaigalukku menu irukkaa?
  • Do you have any vegan dishes? - Unga kitta edhavathu vegan dishes irukkaa?
  • Can I have the bill, please? - Enakku bill kudukkalamaa?
  • Is there a service charge included? - Indha bill-la service charge irukkaa?
  • Can I pay by card? - Enakku card-la payment panna mudiyumaa?
  • Do you serve alcohol? - Inga alcohol kedaikkumaa?
  • Where is the restroom? - Restroom engirukku?
  • Can I reserve a table? - Enakku table reserve panna mudiyumaa?
  • Is there a buffet option? - Inga buffet option irukkaa?
  • Can I have some extra napkins? - Enakku konjam extra napkins kudukkalamaa?
  • Is the food halal? - Inga sapadu halal-a irukkaa?
  • What are your opening hours? - Unga opening hours enna?
  • Can I have this dish without onions? - Indha dish-a vengayam illama seyyalaama?
  • Do you offer home delivery? - Neenga home delivery seyyuringala?
  • Do you have gluten-free options? - Unga kitta gluten-free options irukkaa?
  • Can I have a bottle of mineral water? - Enakku oru bottle mineral water kudukkalamaa?
  • Is there a waiting time for a table? - Table kidaikka wait panna vendiyaa?
  • Can you split the bill? - Neenga bill-a split panna mudiyumaa?
  • What is the signature dish here? - Inga signature dish enna?
  • Do you serve dessert? - Neenga dessert serve seyyuringalaa?
  • Can I get a takeaway box? - Enakku oru takeaway box kudukkalamaa?
  • Do you have a salad bar? - Inga salad bar irukkaa?
  • Is there a dress code? - Inga dress code irukkaa?
  • Do you have outdoor seating? - Unga kitta outdoor seating irukkaa?
  • Can I see the drinks menu? - Enakku drinks menu kaamikkalamaa?
  • Can I get some extra ketchup? - Enakku konjam extra ketchup kudukkalamaa?
  • Do you accept UPI payments? - Neenga UPI payment accept pannuringalaa?
  • Is there any discount for students? - Students-kku edhavathu discount irukkaa?
  • Can I change my order? - Enakku order-a change panna mudiyumaa?
  • What time does the kitchen close? - Kitchen eppo close aagum?
  • Can I get a quiet table? - Enakku oru quiet table kidaikkuma?
  • Do you offer group bookings? - Neenga group bookings seyyuringalaa?
  • What drinks do you have? - Unga kitta enna drinks irukku?
  • Is there free Wi-Fi here? - Inga free Wi-Fi irukkaa?
  • Do you have a bar counter? - Unga kitta bar counter irukkaa?
  • Can you pack this food? - Neenga indha sapadu pack panna mudiyumaa?
  • Do you provide high chairs for kids? - Kuzhandhaigalukku high chair kudukkuringalaa?
  • Is tipping mandatory? - Tipping avasiyamaa?
  • Do you have live music? - Inga live music irukkaa?
  • Can I order from the chef’s special? - Enakku chef’s special-la irundhu order panna mudiyumaa?
  • Is this a family-friendly restaurant? - Ithu family-friendly restaurant-a?
