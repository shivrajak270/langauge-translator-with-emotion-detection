Common Conversations in a Restaurant

Common Conversations in a Restaurant:

  • Will it go to Delhi? - Delhi javse?
  • How much to go to the station? - Station sudhi ketlu lagse?
  • Are you going to the market? - Tame market javso?
  • Take me to Connaught Place. - Mane Connaught Place lai chalo.
  • How far is it from here? - Ahiya thi ketlu door che?
  • Do you have change for 500? - Tamari pase 500 nu chhutta che?
  • Can I sit in the front? - Hu agal besi shakun?
  • How much will you charge to go to the airport? - Airport sudhi javanu ketlu levana?
  • Stop at the signal. - Signal par roko.
  • I’ll get off here. - Hu ahiya utarish.
  • Can you take me to the metro station? - Tame mane metro station lai jasho?
  • What time do you close? - Tamara kaam band karyu ketla vagye che?
  • Is there a shortcut? - Koi shortcut che?
  • Will you wait here for me? - Tamari pase mari raha karsho?
  • How long will it take to reach? - Tya sudhi pahochva ketlu samay lagse?
  • Can you speed up a bit? - Tame thodi speed vadhari shako?
  • Where is the nearest hospital? - Najik nu hospital kya che?
  • I want to go to the bus stand. - Mane bus stand javu che.
  • Can you stop at the next corner? - Agal na modhe roki shako?
  • I forgot my wallet. - Mara pase mari wallet bhuli gayu.
  • Is it safe to travel at night? - Ratri ma travel karvu surakshit che?
  • How many people can sit here? - Ahiya ketla loko besi shake?
  • Do you have a mobile charger? - Tamari pase mobile charger che?
  • I want to get off at the next stop. - Mane agal na stop par utarvu che.
  • Can you make it a little cheaper? - Thodu sasto kari shako?
  • What’s your fare to the airport? - Airport sudhi ketlu fare che?
  • I’ll pay by card. - Hu card thi payment karish.
  • Can you take me to the nearest hotel? - Mane najik na hotel sudhi lai jasho?
  • How much for a round trip? - Ek round trip nu ketlu?
  • Do you take longer routes? - Tame lambu rasto levo cho?
  • Is there a rickshaw stand nearby? - Najik koi rickshaw stand che?
  • Do you go by meter? - Tame meter thi chalto cho?
  • Do you have a seat belt? - Tamari pase seat belt che?
  • Can I bring my pet with me? - Hu mara pet ne sathe lai javi shaku?
  • Can I get a receipt for the fare? - Mane fare ni receipt mali shake?
  • What time do you start work? - Tamara kaam ketla vagya thi sharu thay che?
  • Is the rickshaw available 24 hours? - Rickshaw 24 hours available che?
  • How much for a ride to the city? - City sudhi javanu ketlu fare che?
  • Are you available for a return trip? - Tame return trip mate available cho?
  • Do you have air conditioning? - Tamari pase AC che?
