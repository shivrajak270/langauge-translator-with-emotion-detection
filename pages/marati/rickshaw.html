Conversation in Auto-Rickshaw

Common Conversations in Auto-Rickshaw:

  • He Punyakade jate ka? - Will it go to Pune?
  • Stationla jayla kiti lagel? - How much to go to the station?
  • Tumhi marketla jatay ka? - Are you going to the market?
  • Mala MG Roadvar nyayche aahe. - Take me to MG Road.
  • Ithun kiti antar aahe? - How far is it from here?
  • Tumchyakade 500 che sute aahet ka? - Do you have change for 500?
  • Mee samor basu shakto ka? - Can I sit in the front?
  • Airportla jayla kiti hoil? - How much to go to the airport?
  • Signal var thamba. - Stop at the signal.
  • Mee ithech utarato. - I’ll get off here.
  • Metro station paryant gheu shakal ka? - Can you take me to the metro station?
  • Tumhi ithe thambal ka? - Will you wait for me here?
  • Ya rastyane jato ka? - Are you going this way?
  • Kitva vel lagel pohchayla? - How long will it take to reach?
  • Zara vegane jaun shakal ka? - Can you go a bit faster?
  • Zavalche hospital kuthe aahe? - Where is the nearest hospital?
  • Mala bus stand paryant jayche aahe. - I want to go to the bus stand.
  • Pudcha kona par thamba. - Can you stop at the next corner?
  • Airportla jayla kiti bhadel? - How much will you charge to go to the airport?
  • Mee cardane bharel. - I’ll pay by card.
  • ATM paryant gheu shakal ka? - Can you take me to the nearest ATM?
  • He rasta yogya aahe ka? - Is this the right way to go?
  • Krupa karun mukhya rastyane ja. - Please go through the main road.
  • Zavalcha mall kuthe aahe? - Can you take me to the nearest mall?
  • Round tripla kitva lagel? - How much for a round trip?
  • Ratri pravas surakshit aahe ka? - Is it safe to travel at night?
  • Mee lajli aahe, zara jaldi jau shakal ka? - I’m in a hurry, can you go faster?
  • Tumhi mala gate par utaru shakal ka? - Can you drop me at the gate?
  • Ek vyakti par kitva? - How much for one person?
  • Tumchyakade mobile charger aahe ka? - Do you have a mobile charger?
  • Javalchya restaurant par kiti door aahe? - How far is the nearest restaurant?
  • Mee airport la laghvat pahoyto. - I need to reach the airport quickly.
  • Tumhi ithech raho shakal ka? - Can you stop here?
  • Lambi yatra sathi upalabdha ahat ka? - Are you available for a long trip?
  • Zara sasta karnar ka? - Can you make it cheaper?
  • Mee majha pet gheu shakel ka? - Can I bring my pet along?
  • Pudchya stop var utarna aahe. - I need to get off at the next stop.
  • Krupa karun mala ithech thambayla vahate ka. - Please wait for me here.
  • Tumhi kam kadhi suru karta? - What time do you start work?
  • Tumhi station paryant jaata ka? - Do you go to the station?
  • Tumhi veglya rastyane jau shakat ka? - Can you take a different route?
  • Mage kitne lok basu shaktat? - How many people can sit in the back?
  • Mee driver sobat basu shakto ka? - Can I sit with the driver?
  • Tumhi mala najdeekchya hospital par gheu shakat ka? - Can you take me to the nearest hospital?
  • He sarvat lahan rasta aahe ka? - Is this the shortest route?
  • Tumhi market jaata ka? - Are you going to the market?
  • Tumhi kiraya zara kami karu shakat ka? - Can you reduce the fare a little?
  • Tumchyakade change aahe ka? - Do you have change?
  • Tumhi lahan rastyane jaat ahat ka? - Are you taking the shorter route?
  • Tumhi kuthun ahat? - Where are you from?
