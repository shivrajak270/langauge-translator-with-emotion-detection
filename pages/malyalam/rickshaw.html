Conversation in Auto-Rickshaw

Common Conversations in Auto-Rickshaw:

    • Will it go to Delhi? - Ith Delhi-yilekku pokumo?
    • How much to go to the station? - Station-il poykkan ethra aakum?
    • Are you going to the market? - Ningal market-il pokunnundo?
    • Take me to Connaught Place. - Enne Connaught Place-il kootiye kootikko.
    • How far is it from here? - Ivide ninn athu ethra dooram aanu?
    • Do you have change for 500? - Ningal 500-ile chilla undakumo?
    • Can I sit in the front? - Njan munnil irikkatte?
    • How much will you charge to go to the airport? - Airport-il ethumpol ethra vanganam?
    • Stop at the signal. - Signal-il nirthu.
    • I’ll get off here. - Njan ivide irangum.
    • Can you take me to the metro station? - Ningal enne metro station-il etukko?
    • What time do you close? - Ningal ethra manikku adakkunnu?
    • Is there a shortcut? - Oru shortcut undakumo?
    • Will you wait here for me? - Ningal ivide enna kathu nilkkumo?
    • How long will it take to reach? - Ethra samayam edukkum ethean?
    • Can you speed up a bit? - Ningal kurachu vegam chothiyo?
    • Where is the nearest hospital? - Etu adutha hospital evidae aanu?
    • I want to go to the bus stand. - Enikku bus stand-il pokanam.
    • Can you stop at the next corner? - Ningal adutha mukalil nirthumo?
    • I forgot my wallet. - Ente wallet marannu.
    • Is it safe to travel at night? - Raathri travel cheyyan surakshitham ano?
    • How many people can sit here? - Ivide ethra per irikkam?
    • Do you have a mobile charger? - Ningal oru mobile charger undakumo?
    • I want to get off at the next stop. - Enikku adutha stop-il iranganam.
    • Can you make it a little cheaper? - Idakku kurachu vilakku cheyyumo?
    • What’s your fare to the airport? - Airport-il ethaan rate ethra aanu?
    • I’ll pay by card. - Njan card kond pay cheyyum.
    • Can you take me to the nearest hotel? - Etu adutha hotel-ilekku kootiye pokumo?
    • How much for a round trip? - Round trip-in ethra akum?
    • Do you take longer routes? - Ningal valutha vazhikal eduthum undo?
    • Is there a rickshaw stand nearby? - Etu adutha rickshaw stand evidae undu?
    • Do you go by meter? - Ningal meter-anusarich pokumo?
    • Do you have a seat belt? - Seat belt undakumo?
    • Can I bring my pet with me? - Njan ente pet koode eduthu varatte?
    • Can I get a receipt for the fare? - Rate-in receipt kittumo?
    • What time do you start work? - Ningal work ethra manikku thudangunnu?
    • Is the rickshaw available 24 hours? - Rickshaw 24 hours available ano?
    • How much for a ride to the city? - City-ilekk ethra charge cheyyum?
    • Are you available for a return trip? - Return trip-in available aano?
    • Do you have air conditioning? - AC undakumo?
