Conversation in Auto-Rickshaw

Common Conversations in Auto-Rickshaw:

  • Will it go to Delhi? - Idhu Delhi-kku pogumaa?
  • How much to go to the station? - Station-kku pogum velai evvalavu?
  • Are you going to the market? - Neenga market-ku poguringalaa?
  • Take me to Connaught Place. - Ennai Connaught Place-kku kondu pogunga.
  • How far is it from here? - Inga irundhu evvalavu dhooram?
  • Do you have change for 500? - Unga kitta 500-oda change irukkaa?
  • Can I sit in the front? - Naan munnaadi utkaaralamaa?
  • How much will you charge to go to the airport? - Airport-kku pogum velai evvalavu?
  • Stop at the signal. - Signal-la niruthunga.
  • I’ll get off here. - Naan inga iranguren.
  • Can you take me to the metro station? - Neenga enna metro station-kku kondu poguringalaa?
  • What time do you close? - Neenga enna neram mudikkuringa?
  • Is there a shortcut? - Edhavathu shortcut irukkaa?
  • Will you wait here for me? - Neenga inga enakku wait pannuveengalaa?
  • How long will it take to reach? - Pahakka evvalavu neram aagum?
  • Can you speed up a bit? - Konjam seekiram otta mudiyumaa?
  • Where is the nearest hospital? - Arugilulla hospital enga irukku?
  • I want to go to the bus stand. - Enakku bus stand-kku poganum.
  • Can you stop at the next corner? - Neenga adutha modhila nirutha mudiyumaa?
  • I forgot my wallet. - En purse-a marandhutten.
  • Is it safe to travel at night? - Raathiri travel panna safe-aa?
  • How many people can sit here? - Inga evlo per utkaaralaam?
  • Do you have a mobile charger? - Unga kitta mobile charger irukkaa?
  • I want to get off at the next stop. - Enakku adutha stop-la iranganum.
  • Can you make it a little cheaper? - Konjam sasta pannuveengalaa?
  • What’s your fare to the airport? - Airport-ku pogum velai evvalavu?
  • I’ll pay by card. - Naan card-la pay panren.
  • Can you take me to the nearest hotel? - Neenga enna arugilulla hotel-kku kondu pogalaama?
  • How much for a round trip? - Round trip-kku evvalavu velai?
  • Do you take longer routes? - Neenga lamba rasta edukuringalaa?
  • Is there a rickshaw stand nearby? - Inga pakkathula rickshaw stand irukkaa?
  • Do you go by meter? - Neenga meter-la poguringalaa?
  • Do you have a seat belt? - Unga kitta seat belt irukkaa?
  • Can I bring my pet with me? - Naan en pet-a kooda kondu vara mudiyumaa?
  • Can I get a receipt for the fare? - Enakku kiraye-ku receipt kudukkuringalaa?
  • What time do you start work? - Neenga enna nerathula vela start pannuringa?
  • Is the rickshaw available 24 hours? - Indha rickshaw 24 mani neram available-aa?
  • How much for a ride to the city? - Sheher-kku pogum velai evvalavu?
  • Are you available for a return trip? - Neenga wapasiku available irukeengalaa?
  • Do you have air conditioning? - Unga rickshaw-la AC irukkaa?
