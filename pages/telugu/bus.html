    Conversation on a Bus

    Common Conversations on a Bus:

    • E bus yekkada velthundi? - Where does this bus go?
    • Ticket charge entha? - What is the ticket fare?
    • Next stop enti? - What is the next stop?
    • Meeru change dorikutha? - Do you have change?
    • Seat reserve cheyyali ante ela? - How can I reserve a seat?
    • Bus timing enti? - What are the bus timings?
    • Stop lo signal cheyyali? - Do I need to signal at the stop?
    • Driver ni contact cheyyali. - I need to contact the driver.
    • AC bus aa non-AC? - Is this an AC bus or non-AC?
    • Emergency exit ekkada undi? - Where is the emergency exit?
    • E bus direct ga velluthunda? - Does this bus go directly?
    • Standing allowed aa? - Is standing allowed?
    • Conductor daggara tickets unnaya? - Can I get tickets from the conductor?
    • Bus stop ki daggara ga unda? - Is the stop nearby?
    • Luggage storage facility unda? - Is there a luggage storage facility?
    • Mall stop ki cheppandi. - Let me know when the stop for the mall comes.
    • Return ticket ki ela apply cheyyali? - How to apply for a return ticket?
    • Lost item gurinchi evarini adagali? - Who should I ask about a lost item?
    • Window seat available unda? - Is a window seat available?
    • E bus airport ki velthunda? - Does this bus go to the airport?
    • Online ticket book chesavara? - Did you book the ticket online?
    • Bus full ga unda? - Is the bus full?
    • Ladies seat unda? - Is there a ladies' seat available?
    • Long journey ki comfort unda? - Is this bus comfortable for a long journey?
    • Bus breakdown aipoyindaa? - Has the bus broken down?
    • Crowd thaggutundaa? - Will the crowd reduce?
    • Bus stop ki vellali ante ela? - How do I get to the bus stop?
    • Time ki bus vosthunda? - Will the bus arrive on time?
    • Luggage bay lo place unda? - Is there space in the luggage bay?
    • Tickets booking helpline number cheppandi. - Please provide the ticket booking helpline number.
    • Bus slow ga velthundi? - Is the bus going slowly?
    • Ticket price lo discount unda? - Is there a discount on the ticket price?
    • E bus route correct aa? - Is this the correct route for the bus?
    • Bus lo charging point unda? - Is there a charging point on the bus?
    • Overnight journey ki safe aa? - Is it safe for an overnight journey?
    • Bus lo restroom facilities unnaya? - Are there restroom facilities on the bus?
    • Driver speed thaggutaraa? - Can the driver reduce the speed?
    • Seats adjust cheyyachu? - Can the seats be adjusted?
    • E bus lo snacks available unnaya? - Are snacks available on this bus?
    • Wi-Fi password cheppandi. - Please share the Wi-Fi password.
    • Break ki time untundaa? - Will there be a break during the journey?
    • Journey lo delay unda? - Will there be a delay during the journey?
    • Reserved seat lo yavaru undaru kada? - Is anyone else sitting in the reserved seat?
    • Journey entha time paduthundi? - How long will the journey take?
    • Bus terminal daggara unda? - Is the bus terminal nearby?
    • Luggage receipt dorikuthundaa? - Will I get a receipt for my luggage?
