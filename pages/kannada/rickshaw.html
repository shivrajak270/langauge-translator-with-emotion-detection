Conversation in Auto-Rickshaw

Common Conversations in Rickshaw

  • Idhu Bangalore hogutha? - Will it go to Bangalore?
  • Station ge hogalu hege? - How much to go to the station?
  • Neevu market hogtha idiya? - Are you going to the market?
  • Naanu MG Road ge hogbeku. - Take me to MG Road.
  • Ivaga idhinda hegiddha doorava ide? - How far is it from here?
  • 500 rupayigalu change idhira? - Do you have change for 500?
  • Naanu front nalli koorchu baardhe? - Can I sit in the front?
  • Airport ge hogalu hege? - How much to go to the airport?
  • Signal nalli thagodu. - Stop at the signal.
  • Naan illi inna uttharane. - I’ll get off here.
  • Metro station ge hogalu nanage help maadtha idhira? - Can you take me to the metro station?
  • Neenu yaarigoo samaya tarabeda? - What time do you close?
  • Shortcut idhira? - Is there a shortcut?
  • Naan ivaga illi barthini, neevu thagodu? - Will you wait for me here?
  • Pahuchnege kitna samaya lagta hai? - How long will it take to reach?
  • Neevu sughama vaa nirodha ge yaatakalu naadya himsa? - Can you speed up a bit?
  • Swasthya illi dooravadhige agida koorisalu? - Where is the nearest hospital?
  • Naan bus stand ge koodalu. - I want to go to the bus stand.
  • Koottha sthana par thagodu? - Can you stop at the next corner?
  • Airport ge hogalu kaiyalu hege? - How much will you charge to go to the airport?
  • Naan card nimagide baralu. - I’ll pay by card.
  • Naan ATM khadga ndhige heve? - Can you take me to the nearest ATM?
  • Ee rasta sariyaddu? - Is this the right way to go?
  • Kripya mukhya rasta nalli hogi. - Please go through the main road.
  • Naan mall ge hogalu? - Can you take me to the nearest mall?
  • Round trip kitna hoga? - How much for a round trip?
  • Raati yatra surakshit idheya? - Is it safe to travel at night?
  • Naan sheeghra barbeku, neevu bekagiddare? - I’m in a hurry, can you go faster?
  • Kya aap mujhe gate ke pass utar sakte ho? - Can you drop me at the gate?
  • Ondu vyakti ge kitna? - How much for one person?
  • Kya aapke paas mobile charger hai? - Do you have a mobile charger?
  • Sabse najdeeki restaurant kitni door hai? - How far is the nearest restaurant?
  • Naan airport nalli avega. - I need to reach the airport quickly.
  • Kya aap yahan rukh sakte hain? - Can you stop here?
  • Lambi yatra ke liye aap available hai? - Are you available for a long trip?
  • Kya ise thoda sasta kar sakte ho? - Can you make it cheaper?
  • Kya main apna pet le sakta hoon? - Can I bring my pet along?
  • Agli stop par mujhe utarna hai. - I need to get off at the next stop.
  • Kripya mere liye yahan intezaar karo. - Please wait for me here.
  • Tum kaam kab shuru karte ho? - What time do you start work?
  • Kya aap station tak jaate ho? - Do you go to the station?
  • Kya aap doosra rasta le sakte ho? - Can you take a different route?
  • Pichhe kitne log baith sakte hain? - How many people can sit in the back?
  • Kya main driver ke saath baith sakta hoon? - Can I sit with the driver?
  • Kya aap mujhe sabse najdeeki hospital le ja sakte ho? - Can you take me to the nearest hospital?
  • Kya yeh sabse chhota rasta hai? - Is this the shortest route?
  • Kya aap market ja rahe ho? - Are you going to the market?
  • Kya aap kiraya thoda kam kar sakte ho? - Can you reduce the fare a little?
  • Apne paas change hai? - Do you have change?
  • Kya aap chhota rasta le rahe ho? - Are you taking the shorter route?
  • Aap kaha se ho? - Where are you from?
