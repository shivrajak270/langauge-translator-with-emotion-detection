Conversation in Auto-Rickshaw

Common Conversations in Auto-Rickshaw:

  • Will it go to Delhi? - Delhi jabe ki?
  • How much to go to the station? - Station-e jawar bhara koto?
  • Are you going to the market? - Apni ki bazar-e jachhen?
  • Take me to Connaught Place. - Amake Connaught Place-e niye cholen.
  • How far is it from here? - Ekhane theke koto dur?
  • Do you have change for 500? - Apnar kachhe 500 takar khuchro ache ki?
  • Can I sit in the front? - Ami ki samne boshte pari?
  • How much will you charge to go to the airport? - Airport-e jawar bhara koto?
  • Stop at the signal. - Signal-e thaman.
  • I’ll get off here. - Ami ekhanei nambo.
  • Can you take me to the metro station? - Apni ki amake metro station-e niye jete parben?
  • What time do you close? - Apnar shesh shomoy koto ta?
  • Is there a shortcut? - Kono shortcut ache ki?
  • Will you wait here for me? - Apni ki amar jonno ekhane opekkha korben?
  • How long will it take to reach? - Pohachte koto shomoy lagbe?
  • Can you speed up a bit? - Apni ki ektu taratari chalate parben?
  • Where is the nearest hospital? - Sabche kachher hospital kothay?
  • I want to go to the bus stand. - Amake bus stand-e niye cholen.
  • Can you stop at the next corner? - Apni ki agami moR-e thamte parben?
  • I forgot my wallet. - Amar wallet bhule gechi.
  • Is it safe to travel at night? - Raat-e yatra kora ki nirapod?
  • How many people can sit here? - Ekhane koto jon boshete pare?
  • Do you have a mobile charger? - Apnar kachhe mobile charger ache ki?
  • I want to get off at the next stop. - Ami agami stop-e nambo.
  • Can you make it a little cheaper? - Ektu kom bhara hobe ki?
  • What’s your fare to the airport? - Airport-e jawar bhara koto?
  • I’ll pay by card. - Ami card-e payment korbo.
  • Can you take me to the nearest hotel? - Apni ki amake sabche kachher hotel-e niye jete parben?
  • How much for a round trip? - Round trip-er bhara koto hobe?
  • Do you take longer routes? - Apni ki lamba rasta dhoren?
  • Is there a rickshaw stand nearby? - Kachhe kono rickshaw stand ache ki?
  • Do you go by meter? - Apni ki meter-e chalen?
  • Do you have a seat belt? - Apnar kachhe seat belt ache ki?
  • Can I bring my pet with me? - Ami ki amar paaltu posha niye aste pari?
  • Can I get a receipt for the fare? - Ami ki bhara-r raseed pete pari?
  • What time do you start work? - Apnar kajer shomoy kokhon theke?
  • Is the rickshaw available 24 hours? - Rickshaw ki 24 ghonta available?
  • How much for a ride to the city? - Sheher-e jawar bhara koto?
  • Are you available for a return trip? - Apni ki wapsi yatra-r jonno available?
  • Do you have air conditioning? - Apnar rickshaw-te ki AC ache?
