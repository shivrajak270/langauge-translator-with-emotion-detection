Conversation in Bus

Common Conversations in Bus

  • Ya busne Pune la jaycha aahe ka? - Does this bus go to Pune?
  • Next stop kuthe aahe? - Where is the next stop?
  • Tumhala ticket kuthe gheu? - Where should I buy the ticket?
  • Ya busche time table kuthe aahe? - Where is the timetable for this bus?
  • Bus madhe jaga aahe ka? - Is there a seat available on this bus?
  • Ya busne station paryant jaycha aahe. - I need to go to the station on this bus.
  • Mala pudhcha stopla utarayecha aahe. - I need to get off at the next stop.
  • Bus madhe kuthun chadave? - Where should I board the bus?
  • Tumhala kitne lagtil ticket sathi? - How much does the ticket cost?
  • Driver cha phone number milu shakto ka? - Can I get the driver's phone number?
  • Ya busne Mumbai la jaycha aahe ka? - Does this bus go to Mumbai?
  • Last stop kuthe aahe? - Where is the last stop?
  • Mala zavalchya bus stop var jaycha aahe. - I need to go to the nearest bus stop.
  • Ya bus madhe AC aahe ka? - Does this bus have AC?
  • Tumhala pass dakhvaycha aahe ka? - Do I need to show the pass?
  • Mala khidkit basu shakto ka? - Can I sit by the window?
  • Bus la station paryant pohchayla kitva vel lagel? - How long will it take for the bus to reach the station?
  • He bus kasli seva aahe? - What type of service is this bus?
  • Tumhi mala samorachya stop var utaru shakat ka? - Can you drop me at the stop ahead?
  • Bus madhe khadya padartha allowed aahet ka? - Are snacks allowed on the bus?
  • Driver la mala stopla sangaycha aahe ka? - Do I need to tell the driver to stop?
  • Ya bus madhe WiFi aahe ka? - Does this bus have WiFi?
  • Ticket online book karu shakto ka? - Can I book the ticket online?
  • Tumhala cash payment houn shakt ka? - Can I pay in cash?
  • Bus ticket refund houn shakto ka? - Can I get a refund for the bus ticket?
  • Tumhi last stop var thambnar ka? - Will you stop at the last stop?
  • Tumcha pass check kon karnar? - Who will check the pass?
  • Tumchyakade luggage rakhan sathi jaga aahe ka? - Is there space to keep luggage?
  • Ya bus madhe charging point aahe ka? - Does this bus have a charging point?
  • Ticket sathi card use karu shakto ka? - Can I use a card to pay for the ticket?
  • Bus kiti velane yenar? - When will the bus arrive?
  • Bus schedule kuthe bhetel? - Where can I find the bus schedule?
  • He stop chukla tar? - What if I miss my stop?
  • Bus service la delay aahe ka? - Is the bus service delayed?
  • Tumhi emergency exit cha marg mahit aahe ka? - Do you know the emergency exit route?
  • Bus la fuel bharayla kitva vel lagel? - How long will it take to refuel the bus?
  • Driver experienced aahe ka? - Is the driver experienced?
  • Tumhi he stop sangshil ka? - Can you let me know when my stop arrives?
  • Bus tickets bulk madhe discount aahet ka? - Are there discounts for bulk tickets?
  • Bus madhe pet allowed aahe ka? - Are pets allowed on the bus?
  • Ya bus service cha customer care number milu shakto ka? - Can I get the customer care number for this bus service?
  • Bus madhe sleeper berth aahe ka? - Does the bus have sleeper berths?
  • Ya bus madhe konthi suvidha available aahet? - What facilities are available on this bus?
  • Bus madhe women reserved seat kuthe aahe? - Where is the reserved seat for women?
  • Ya bus madhe safe aahe ka? - Is it safe to travel on this bus?
  • Bus la yatrianchi limit aahe ka? - Is there a limit to the number of passengers?
  • Tumhi bus la abhyasarthi pass gheu shakat ka? - Can you take a student pass for this bus?
