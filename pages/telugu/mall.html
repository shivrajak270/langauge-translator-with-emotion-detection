Conversation in a Mall

  • Discount sale ekkada jarugutundi? - Where is the discount sale happening?
  • Meeru gift wrapping chestara? - Do you offer gift wrapping?
  • Trial room ekkada undi? - Where is the trial room?
  • Idi last price aa? - Is this the final price?
  • Ee item lo different colors unnaya? - Are there different colors available for this item?
  • Food court ekkada undi? - Where is the food court?
  • Parking fee entha? - How much is the parking fee?
  • Elevator ekkada undi? - Where is the elevator?
  • Idi return policy enti? - What is the return policy for this?
  • Customer service desk ekkada undi? - Where is the customer service desk?
  • Meeru coupons accept chestara? - Do you accept coupons?
  • ATM ekkada undi? - Where is the ATM?
  • Cinema tickets ikkada konagala? - Can I buy movie tickets here?
  • Washroom ekkada undi? - Where is the washroom?
  • Naku help desk daggara velali. - I need to go to the help desk.
  • Ee floor lo kids section unda? - Is there a kids' section on this floor?
  • Loyalty card apply cheyyali. - I want to apply for a loyalty card.
  • Mall closing time enti? - What time does the mall close?
  • Idi delivery ki available aa? - Is this available for delivery?
  • Security office ekkada undi? - Where is the security office?
  • Meeru online payment accept chestara? - Do you accept online payments?
  • Mee daggara gift cards unnaya? - Do you have gift cards available?
  • Lost and found section ekkada undi? - Where is the lost and found section?
  • Meeru discounts ki eligible aa? - Am I eligible for discounts?
  • Nenu size exchange cheyyali. - I need to exchange this for a different size.
  • Food court lo vegetarian options unnaya? - Are there vegetarian options in the food court?
  • Play area ekkada undi? - Where is the play area?
  • First floor ki ela veltaru? - How do I go to the first floor?
  • Spill cleanup chesara? - Can you clean up the spill?
  • Discounts ki coupons unnaya? - Are there coupons for discounts?
  • Refund process enti? - What is the refund process?
  • Parking lo vacant slots unnaya? - Are there any vacant slots in the parking lot?
  • Free Wi-Fi password enti? - What is the free Wi-Fi password?
  • Customer care number chepthara? - Can you share the customer care number?
  • Idi emi brand undi? - What brand is this?
  • Ikkada festive offers unnaya? - Are there any festive offers here?
  • Mall map ekkada dorukutundi? - Where can I get a map of the mall?

  • Home delivery ki charges enti? - What are the charges for home delivery?

  • Nenu bill counters ki ela vellali? - How do I get to the billing counters?
  • Mee daggara shopping bags dorukutaya? - Do you have shopping bags available?
  • Food court lo table free undi? - Is there a free table in the food court?
  • Ee dress ki accessories kavali. - I need accessories for this dress.
  • Enni items ki discount apply chestaru? - How many items are eligible for the discount?
  • Parking ticket misaya, ye alternative undi? - I lost my parking ticket; what can I do?
  • Luggage storage facility ekkada undi? - Where is the luggage storage facility?
  • Fashion section lo new arrivals unnaya? - Are there new arrivals in the fashion section?
