Conversation in Auto-Rickshaw

Common Conversations in Auto-Rickshaw:

  • Will it go to Delhi? - Kya Delhi chaloge?
  • How much to go to the station? - Station jaane ka kitna hoga?
  • Are you going to the market? - Kya aap bazaar ja rahe ho?
  • Take me to Connaught Place. - Mujhe Connaught Place le chalo.
  • How far is it from here? - Yahan se kitni door hai?
  • Do you have change for 500? - Kya aapke paas 500 ka chutta hai?
  • Can I sit in the front? - Kya main aage baith sakta hoon?
  • How much will you charge to go to the airport? - Airport jaane ka kitna loge?
  • Stop at the signal. - Signal par rokna.
  • I’ll get off here. - Main yahan utar jaunga.
  • Can you take me to the metro station? - Kya aap mujhe metro station tak le ja sakte ho?
  • What time do you close? - Aap kitne baje band karte ho?
  • Is there a shortcut? - Kya koi shortcut hai?
  • Will you wait here for me? - Kya tum mere liye yahan intezaar karoge?
  • How long will it take to reach? - Pahuchne mein kitna samay lagega?
  • Can you speed up a bit? - Kya aap thoda tez chala sakte ho?
  • Where is the nearest hospital? - Sabse najdeeki hospital kahaan hai?
  • I want to go to the bus stand. - Mujhe bus stand jaana hai.
  • Can you stop at the next corner? - Kya aap agle mod par rok sakte ho?
  • I forgot my wallet. - Mera purse bhool gaya.
  • Is it safe to travel at night? - Kya raat mein yatra karna surakshit hai?
  • How many people can sit here? - Yahan kitne log baith sakte hain?
  • Do you have a mobile charger? - Kya aapke paas mobile charger hai?
  • I want to get off at the next stop. - Mujhe agli stop par utarna hai.
  • Can you make it a little cheaper? - Kya aap ise thoda sasta kar sakte ho?
  • What’s your fare to the airport? - Airport ka kiraya kitna hai?
  • I’ll pay by card. - Main card se bhugtan karunga.
  • Can you take me to the nearest hotel? - Kya aap mujhe najdeeki hotel tak le ja sakte ho?
  • How much for a round trip? - Round trip ka kitna hoga?
  • Do you take longer routes? - Kya aap lamba rasta lete ho?
  • Is there a rickshaw stand nearby? - Kya yahaan paas mein rickshaw stand hai?
  • Do you go by meter? - Kya aap meter se chalte ho?
  • Do you have a seat belt? - Kya aapke paas seat belt hai?
  • Can I bring my pet with me? - Kya main apna paaltu jaanwar le aa sakta hoon?
  • Can I get a receipt for the fare? - Kya mujhe kiraye ki raseed mil sakti hai?
  • What time do you start work? - Aap kaam kab shuru karte ho?
  • Is the rickshaw available 24 hours? - Kya yeh rickshaw 24 ghante available hai?
  • How much for a ride to the city? - Sheher tak ka kiraya kitna hoga?
  • Are you available for a return trip? - Kya aap wapsi ki yatra ke liye available ho?
  • Do you have air conditioning? - Kya aapke rickshaw mein AC hai?
