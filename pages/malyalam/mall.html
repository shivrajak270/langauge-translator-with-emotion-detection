Common Conversations in a Mall

Common Conversations in a Mall:

  • Where is the nearest mall entrance? - Ellavarum pinnimulla mall praveshanam evideyanu?
  • Can you guide me to the food court? - Ente food court varayulla margam kaanikkumo?
  • Where can I find a clothing store? - Vastravalayam evideyanu kittunnath?
  • Is there a sale going on? - Oru sale nadannu koode?
  • What are the mall's opening hours? - Mall ethra samayam thirakkunnu?
  • Where can I park my car? - Ente veendum evideyanu park cheyyunnathu?
  • Where is the cinema located? - Cinema evideyanu sthithi cheyyunnathu?
  • Can I pay with a card here? - Ingane card kondu payment cheyyamo?
  • Do you have a directory of stores? - Dukanalude directory undooo?
  • Where is the washroom? - Toilet evideyanu?
  • How much does parking cost? - Parking ethrayannu valiya?
  • Is there a kid's play area? - Kuzhchangalude play area undooo?
  • Where can I buy tickets for the movie? - Movie ticket evideyanu kidakkunnath?
  • Can you tell me where the escalator is? - Escalator evideyanu ennu paranju tharamo?
  • Which floor has electronics stores? - Electronics dukanalude floor ethayannu?
  • Where can I find the information desk? - Information desk evideyanu kittunnathu?
  • Is there free Wi-Fi in the mall? - Mallil mukthamaya Wi-Fi undooo?
  • Can you help me find a toy store? - Toy store kandetharaan engilum sahayikkaamo?
  • Where can I get some coffee? - Coffee evideyanu kidakkunnath?
  • Do you have vegetarian food options? - Vegetarian bhakshanangal undooo?
  • Where can I find the grocery section? - Grocery section evideyanu?
  • How can I access the rooftop terrace? - Rooftop terrace varayulla margam ethayannu?
  • Is there an ATM inside the mall? - Mallil oru ATM undooo?
  • Can I get a map of the mall? - Mall kalude map kidakkumo?
  • Are there any events happening today? - Enniku oru event nadakkunnundo?
  • Where can I find accessories? - Accessories evideyanu kidakkunnath?
  • What is the last showtime for the movie? - Movie kalude last show samayam ethayannu?
  • Where is the exit? - Exit evideyanu?
  • Do you have gift wrapping services? - Gift vappan services undooo?
  • Are there any discounts in the mall today? - Enniku mallil discount undooo?
  • Where is the lost and found section? - Lost and found section evideyanu?
  • Do I need to book tickets in advance? - Ticket advance il bookku cheyyendathundo?
  • Can I use a wheelchair here? - Ingane wheelchair upayogikkaamo?
  • Is there a pharmacy in the mall? - Mallil pharmacy undooo?
  • Where can I charge my phone? - Phone charge cheyyunna location evideyanu?
  • Do you have a cloakroom? - Cloakroom undooo?
